The Geek Writers meetings occur on the 4th Wednesday of every month at 6:30pm. Currently we are holding the meetings at the Oklahoma City Coworking Collaborative in beautiful Midtown Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. The meetings are free and we try to provide snacks and drinks for everyone who comes. So if you have a love of writing and consider yourself a geek about it, come on out and join us!

If you want to get the latest news and RSVP for our meetings, see scheduled talks, participate in writing prompts, and meet the other members, check out our Meetup group: http://www.meetup.com/geekwriters/.
